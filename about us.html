ABOUT US


With the MY Food team you are in experienced hands, we have been delivering a delicious way of life for nearly 14 years, so we know achieving your weight loss & health goals is easier with us to help you. Our founder, Nas, can always be found at MY Food HQ, adding her creative input to recipes & supporting our customers with their goals. We pride ourselves in preparing fresh food, using the finest ingredients carefully flavoured with herbs & spices, but without any artificial additives or preservatives. Our food is fresh & must be stored in the fridge, we do not make ambient packet food as our meals are made by & for people who love real food.

Our experienced team of experts create menus & prepare calorie counted meals for weight loss, macro counted meals for people who train & health specific diets, for those with specific dietary needs. If you have any other requirements please get in touch & we will do our best to help you. 14 years on we still love what we do and we are confident that you will too!
